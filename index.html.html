How It Works

Six stages. Under a second.

Every word you speak travels through a real-time AI pipeline and arrives in the listener's language before you've finished your sentence.

1
You Speak

Your voice is captured via WebRTC in 300ms chunks. No delays, no buffering — just natural conversation.

2
AI Transcribes

Whisper AI converts your speech to text in real time, detecting your language automatically with fastText.

3
Instant Translation

DeepL or NLLB-200 translates your words mid-sentence, preserving tone, context, and technical terms.

4
Voice Synthesis

ElevenLabs generates a natural-sounding voice in the listener's language before your sentence is even finished.

5
They Hear You

The translated voice plays in the listener's ear — clear, natural, and in their own language.

6
Live Captions

Dual-script captions appear in both languages simultaneously, giving both parties a written record of every word.
